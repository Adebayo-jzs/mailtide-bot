Privacy Policy

Last updated  ·  June 2026

01

Data Collection & Usage

MailTide requires read-only access to your Gmail account via OAuth2. This access is used solely to scan your inbox for job-related emails — interview invitations, offer letters, rejections, assessments, and application confirmations — and forward relevant notifications to your Telegram account.

We do not read, index, or process any emails outside of job-application context. All classification happens in-memory at the time of scanning and is never written to disk.

MailTide only requests the gmail.readonly scope. It cannot send, delete, or modify any email on your behalf.

02

Data Storage

We do not store your emails or their contents. The only data persisted to our systems is:

  • Your Telegram Chat ID — to route notifications to you.
  • An encrypted OAuth2 refresh token — to maintain your Gmail connection without requiring repeated sign-ins.
  • A last-checked timestamp — so the bot knows where to resume after a restart.

No email bodies, subjects, sender addresses, or snippets are stored at any point.

03

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. The only external services MailTide communicates with are Google Gmail API (to read emails) and Telegram Bot API (to deliver notifications) — both on your behalf and under their respective privacy terms.

04

Data Deletion

You can remove all stored data and disconnect MailTide at any time through either method:

  • Send the /stop command to the bot on Telegram to delete your record from our database.
  • Revoke Gmail access directly from your Google Account Permissions page.

Revoking access or issuing /stop is immediate and permanent. No residual data is retained after either action.

05

Contact

Questions or concerns about this policy? Reach out via Telegram or open an issue on the project repository. We'll respond within 48 hours.
