Features

Everything you need to stay
on top of your job search

A smart layer between your inbox and your chat — filtering noise, categorizing updates, and delivering what matters.

01

Real-Time Polling

Checks your Gmail every 60 seconds. Get notified on Telegram within a minute of receiving a job email.

02

Smart Categorization

Keyword matching classifies emails into 7 categories — offers, interviews, rejections, assessments, and more.

03

AES-256 Encryption

Your Gmail tokens are encrypted with AES-256-GCM at rest. We take your data security seriously.

04

Multi-User Support

Each user connects their own Gmail via OAuth2. The bot handles many users simultaneously with per-user polling.

05

Read-Only Access

MailTide only requests the gmail.readonly scope. It can never send, delete, or modify your emails.

06

Disconnect Anytime

Use /stop to instantly revoke access. Your refresh token and data are permanently deleted.

How It Works

Up and running in three steps

Connect your Gmail, and MailTide handles the rest. No config files, no cron jobs, no hassle.

01

Start the bot on Telegram

Search for MailTide on Telegram and tap /start. You'll get a button to connect your Gmail account via Google's secure OAuth2 flow.

02

Authorize Gmail read access

Sign in with Google and grant read-only access. MailTide scans your last 48 hours of email immediately and sends any job updates it finds.

03

Sit back and get notified

MailTide checks your inbox every 60 seconds. When a new job email arrives, you get a categorized Telegram notification — instantly.
